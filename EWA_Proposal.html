How It Works

Three steps from your Kajabi vault to an instant answer.

📥
Step 1 — Extract
Pull transcripts from Kajabi (native SRT files on Growth/Pro) or Wistia API. Map every transcript to its course → module → lesson.
⚙️
Step 2 — Index
Chunk transcripts into ~500 word segments with overlap. Tag each chunk with its source location. Build the searchable knowledge base.
🤖
Step 3 — Answer
User asks a question. Top matching chunks are retrieved. Gemini AI synthesizes an answer grounded in that material and cites the source.
